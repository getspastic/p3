P3 in the Press

We have been fortunate enough to contribute to some important national conversations, as well drawing the interest of some of the largest names across various business sectors. Below is a sampling of interviews, podcasts, and our contributions to many of the conversations taking place in boardrooms across the country.

Goals DO Come True with Doug Bennett: How to Cut Your Teeth on Your Business Goals with Aaron Stahl (November 7, 2023)

Our founder Aaron sits down with Doug Bennettt the director of Financial Freedom. In this episode of Doug’s podcast 'Goals Do Come True,' Doug and Aaron explore the nuances of setting effective business goals, the paradox of steering by letting go, and mastering the art of achieving small, actionable objectives.

Why My Company Implemented a Paid Sabbatical Program for Employees - Inc.com (February 2, 2023)

Our founder Aaron was asked to contribute to the growing body of wisdom around employee-centric workplaces. He explores implementing a paid sabbatical program at P3 Cost Analysts. Inspired by Tim Ferriss' "The 4-Hour Workweek."

Scaling Up Services with Bruce Eckfeldt — (July 10, 2020)

Scaling Up Services is a podcast with a focused audience of key executives of service-based businesses. Bruce Eckfeldt, performance management coach with 20+ years’ experience working with high growth companies, chats with Aaron about his personal journey both as a founder of a high-growth company and a service provider to many of the same.

Aaron Stahl and P3 Cost Analysts Are Flying High – Arkansas Money and Politics (February 5, 2020

Jeremy Peppas, the founder of Arkansas News Room, asked Aaron to share his learnings as he went from growing up in Mountain Home to founding the high-growth franchise P3 Cost Analysts, traveling to 40 countries, and not only becoming a pilot but also a flight instructor.

Aaron Stahl of P3 Cost Analysts - Bootstrapping In America (December 10, 2019)

Aaron was invited to chat with Kristi Ross, co-CEO Tastytrade on her TastyLive's 'Bootstrapping in America' show. They explore the P3 Cost Analysts business model, its genesis, and the genesis of our franchising program.
