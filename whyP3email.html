Franchise Training

P3 has an initial training program of 40 hours, which is delivered virtually across three weeks, combining live instruction with self-paced components to maximize your flexibility This training includes:
  • Business management with a focus on cost reduction strategy.
  • Compliance and credit-related education.
  • Revenue maximization and profitability planning.
  • Industry expertise from leaders in the cost-reduction arena.
  • Sales and marketing mastery to build a robust pipeline.
  • Guidance on developing strong referral networks.
  • Comprehensive support in prospecting strategy and effectively communicating with decision-makers to maximize sales opportunities.
Operational Assistance

P3 operates with a centralized back office, simplifying your role by handling all auditing, negotiating, and ongoing vendor management. You'll benefit from our dedicated operations team, which assumes the responsibilities of invoicing, collections, and continuous monitoring. This allows you to focus heavily on business development.
