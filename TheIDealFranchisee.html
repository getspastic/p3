Are You Our Next P3 Franchisee?

Here's what it takes to be successful with P3:

  • Sales Acumen: Comfortable navigating low-pressure, cold outreach marketing and B2B sales.
  • Relationship Builder: Skilled in engaging and understanding the needs of potential clients.
  • Learning Agility: Keen to adopt our sales methods through P3's training program.

What Isn't Required?

We are in a complicated busienss. Auditing our client's bills, knowing where to find the likely billing errors or overcharges, and also knowing the most likely negotable points are equal parts art and skill. The good news is, we bring all of that knowlege and execution to the table. As you consider P3 as a business opportunity, you'll here us discuss auditing and billing codes and the like, but you as the franchisee will never be expected to do any part of that side of the business. We handle the entire back-office process, freeing you up to leverage your skills to build the front end.

Commitment and Investment

  • Vision for Change: Ready to make a life change within the next 6 months.
  • Financial Readiness: Ability to invest $50,000 to $100,000 in launching your business.
  • Full-time Dedication: Prepared for a full-time commitment without immediate pressure for profit.

Your Role in P3

As a P3 franchise owner, your main focus will be on driving business growth through proactive outbound B2B sales. You will spend around 80% of your time on this aspect of the business, using your sales skills to secure new clients and expand your network. The remaining 20% of your time will be dedicated to managing client accounts and ensuring their needs are seamlessly met by our operations team.
